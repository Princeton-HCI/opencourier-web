Privacy Policy for the OpenCourier Mobile Client

Data Collection

Most of functionality is performed locally on your device, and will upload to the server you choose to host your account(s). Location data will be collected but not stored.

Local-Only Operation

All interactions—such as browsing accepting, rejecting tasks—are handled directly on your device and with the server your choose. Our app works as a client interface, similar to email apps like Gmail or Outlook, without relaying data.

Third-Party Platforms

When registering to a gig platform, your data is subject to their privacy policies. We do not monitor or access data shared between you and these services.

Permissions and Device Access

The app may request minimal permissions (such as internet access) necessary for core features. We do not access your location, contacts, or other sensitive device information unless explicitly needed and clearly communicated.
